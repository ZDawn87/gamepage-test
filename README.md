## 💡 **Tips & Tricks**

### **🧠 2048 Strategy**
> **Pro Tip**: Keep your highest tile in a corner and build around it!

1. **Corner Strategy**: Choose a corner and never move your highest tile away
2. **Edge Building**: Build your next highest tiles along the edges
3. **Patience Pays**: Don't rush - think two moves ahead
4. **Pattern Recognition**: Learn common tile patterns for efficiency

### **⚔️ Tower Defense Mastery**
> **Pro Tip**: Place towers at path intersections for maximum coverage!

1. **Choke Points**: Identify where enemies bunch up
2. **Range Optimization**: Overlap tower ranges for concentrated fire
3. **Economy Management**: Balance tower building with wave preparation
4. **Path Analysis**: Study enemy routes to predict optimal placement
